SMART BINS


What? Why? How?

One of the major common problems observed in undeveloped as well as in developing nations is waste management. Owing to the rapidly increasing population and urbanization this issue has been the cause of non-hygienic environment and several other problems including but not limited to Bad odor, non-attractive environment and several diseases. Be it disposing house waste in household bins or collection of community waste through Municipal corporation issued bins, dustbins play an Important role and hence, should be managed appropriately. The biggest issue seen with Public Garbage bins is they tend to overflow before due time of next cleaning process according to the cleaning schedule of the Municipal corporation. The main theme of this seminar is focused upon implementing a smart bin system for Efficient garbage disposal.
